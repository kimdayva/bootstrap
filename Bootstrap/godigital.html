What does it mean to go digital?

There are a few things you need in order to be able to plan digitally:

  • tablet with bluetooth pen
  • notetaking application
  • hyperlinked pdf planner
  • willingness to learn

There is no "right" or "wrong" way to plan, and that's true for paper planning and digital planning. With digital planning, you can choose to use your own handwriting with the pen tool or type notes into your planner using the text tool. You can change text colors, sizes, and styles, or you can leave them uniform. You can add images to your digital planner, whether that's stickers - made by you or purchased - or actually digital photos you've taken and saved. Images can be imported to your planner from wherever you choose to store them. They can be resized and moved and used over and over again. Your digital planner can become more like a scrapbook that just another calendar.
