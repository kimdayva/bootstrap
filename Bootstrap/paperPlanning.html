Good old Paper...

There are a number of paper planner companies that have been around for years, and there are paper planner companies that are just hitting the scene. You can find paper planners with monthly, weekly, and daily pages in any and every combination you can imagine. Many planners come bound, whether coiled or hardbound, and others can be pieced together, either in binder rings or discs. It can still be difficult to find some of the more specific trackers in most published planners though.

One of the newer paper planner companies that has taken a direct sales business model is Tula Xii. They are very DIY in nature and have a tracker for just about anything you can think of tracking. They offer a variety of layouts for monthly calendars and weekly calendars too. It's a folio system that uses individual insert booklets that can be swapped out as they are used.
