What's in a digital planner?

A digital planner is a hyperlinked pdf that can be used and annotated using a notetaking app on a mobile device. You can have bullet journals, blank journals, monthly calendars, weekly calendars, daily agendas, meal planning menus, habit trackers, bill trackers, and so much more! They can be used across operating systems; however, not all notetaking programs are compatible across devices.

Digital planners are often designed to look like your favorite paper planners. Some appear to be in binders, while others are coiled. They have tabs for different months and sections, and that's where you will find the hyperlinks to take you to the different pages in your planner.

The beauty of a digital planner is that it's only as heavy as your tablet or phone. There's no messy erasing, and changing plans is as easy as copy and paste. However, as you pile on the notes and decorations, your file size increases, so it may take longer to load as your file size increases.
